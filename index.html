How I Work

I focus on building reliable data systems and analytics that support real decisions, not just outputs.

  • Start with the question: I clarify what decision the data needs to support before building pipelines or dashboards.
  • Design for data quality: I prioritize validation and consistency early, because unreliable data breaks everything downstream.
  • Keep systems simple: I prefer clear data models and maintainable workflows over unnecessary complexity.
  • Communicate clearly: I explain technical work so both technical and non-technical users can act on it.
